To move the fire engine left click in the direction you wish to move in.

To shoot water from the fire engine right click in the direction you want to shoot in.

To replenish health, fuel and water; move to the fire station

Your health, fuel and water is displayed by the bars underneath the fire engine

You can select a different fire engine by left clicking on it.

Your objective to is to destroy all of the alien bases before they destroy all of your fire
engines.
